The 12 Zodiac Animals

The Chinese zodiac signs form a recurring 12-year cycle, and each year is represented by one of 12 animals. 2017 is a year of the Rooster, and 2018 will be a year of the Dog. The year you were born determines your zodiac sign, e.g. if you were born in a year of the Rooster (… 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005…), you will be a Rooster. The animals are present in many cultures, and they are connected to several superstitious activities and beliefs indluding luck, marriage, wealth, personality and so forth!

Please select and click the animal you want to know more about, as each animal comes with interesting facts and personalities!
